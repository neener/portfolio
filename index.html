HEAT DAO
The goal of this project was to secure a first round of funding for Heat DAO. We were tasked to created an in-browser experience that allowed the user to simulate a multi-player universe where you could buy dance moves (as NFTs) for your avatar. I was brought onto the project by Angeline Meitzler to collaborate in creating a 3D environment for users to purchase movement NFTs to dance with other players.

Tech used: Express, React Three Fiber, Solidity
